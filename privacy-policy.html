2.1 Special Permissions and Data for Parental Control

As a parental control application, DiriCode utilizes specific Android permissions and APIs to deliver its core functionalities. We obtain explicit and informed consent from parents/guardians before enabling these features.

Accessibility Service API:

DiriCode uses the Android AccessibilityService API (android.permission.BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE) on the child's device to enable critical parental control features. This API is used for the following purposes:

  • App Blocking: To detect when a child attempts to launch an application designated as blocked by the parent and to prevent its use.
  • Screen Time Limits: To enforce time limits set by the parent on specific applications or overall device usage.
  • Uninstall Protection: To prevent the unauthorized uninstallation of the DiriCode application, ensuring continuous parental oversight.

This service processes app launch events and package names of foreground applications solely for the purpose of triggering blocking actions and enforcing parental rules. It does not collect personal content, modify user settings without consent, or circumvent Android's built-in privacy controls. Parental consent is explicitly obtained in-app before this service is activated.

Usage Stats API ('PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS'):

DiriCode requires the android.permission.PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS permission to collect information about application usage times and patterns on the child's device. This data includes which applications are used and for how long.

  • Purpose: This data is vital for providing parents with comprehensive reports and insights into their child's device activity, enabling them to understand and manage their child's digital habits effectively.
  • Usage: Collected usage data is securely transmitted to our cloud servers (Firebase Firestore) and is linked only to the associated parent's account. It is not shared with third parties for advertising, profiling, or any other unrelated purposes. Parental consent is explicitly obtained in-app before accessing this data.

Location Data (Expanded from point 6):

When the "Find Phone (child location)" feature is enabled by the parent, DiriCode collects the device's precise (and/or coarse) location (using android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION, and android.permission.ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION).

  • Purpose: This data is collected to provide real-time location tracking for the child's device, enabling parents to locate their child for safety and oversight.
  • Usage: Location coordinates and associated timestamps are securely stored on our Firebase Firestore servers and are accessible only to the parent associated with the child's account. This data is not used for advertising or shared with unrelated third parties. Parental consent is explicitly obtained for location tracking.
